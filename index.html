About Us

Northern Uganda Film Awards

The Northern Uganda Film Awards (NUFA) is dedicated to celebrating and promoting the incredible talent in the region’s film industry. We honor the best in storytelling, cinematography, and artistic expression while fostering growth and collaboration within the creative community.
